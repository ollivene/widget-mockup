What we offer

Comprehensive Veterinary Services

From preventive care to complex surgery, our experienced team provides a full range of services for dogs, cats, rabbits, and exotic pets.

Wellness & Check-ups
Annual health exams, vaccinations, flea & worm prevention, and tailored health plans for every life stage.
Soft Tissue Surgery
Neutering, lump removals, wound repairs and a wide range of routine and complex surgical procedures.
Diagnostics & Imaging
In-house digital X-ray, ultrasound, blood analysis, and urine testing for rapid, accurate diagnosis.
Emergency Care
24-hour emergency line with senior vets on call. Don't hesitate — call us immediately if you're worried.
Dental Care
Professional dental scaling, polishing, and extractions under full general anaesthesia with monitoring.
Preventive & Rehab
Microchipping, weight clinics, hydrotherapy referrals, and long-term chronic disease management.
About us

Edinburgh's Neighbourhood Vet Since 2012

A team that genuinely cares

Best Vets Company was founded with a single mission: to give every pet in Edinburgh access to outstanding veterinary care without the stress. We know that visiting the vet can be daunting — for pets and owners alike — and we work hard to make every visit as calm and positive as possible.

Our team of eight qualified vets and twelve nurses brings decades of combined experience in small animal medicine, surgery, and exotic species. We invest continuously in equipment, training, and facilities so your pet always receives evidence-based care.

Common questions

Good to know before you book

Is online booking safe to use?
Yes. All data is sent over an encrypted connection and stored on RCVS-compliant systems. Only our clinical team can see your pet's record.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
Most days we have same-day or next-day slots. The booking screen always shows the soonest available time at the top.
Can I change my booking later?
Of course. Use Manage appointment any time before your visit to reschedule or cancel — no need to call us.
What if my pet has an emergency?
Online booking is for non-urgent visits. If it's an emergency, call our 24h line on 01572 999 000 straight away.
